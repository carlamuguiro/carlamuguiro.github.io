TOPSHOP CHRISTMAS “DEAR TOPSHOP” In partnership with PINTEREST


For Christmas 2015, Topshop launched a campaign on Pinterest entitled “Dear Topshop”, to help consumers create their “ultimate” Christmas gift guide. The campaign allowed consumers to pull together Topshop items across all of the brand’s platforms, from stores and online platforms, to social media and their own gift board. I oversaw the creative direction, including art direction of still life photography, design of the digital “pinnable” gift guide (Pinterest API, beta version), e-mail communication, and social assets.
