TOPSHOP ZINE Biannual magazine


Topshop used to launch a bi-annual magazine and my team worked on the digital version. Once the concept was signed off our role was to create the user experience, as well as to design engaging ways of digesting large amounts of content. The magazine was viewed on iphone, ipad and desktop. Assets were repurposed for international partners and we also created a short video teaser for launch.
