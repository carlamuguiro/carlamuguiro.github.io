TOPSHOP #LIVETRENDS In partnership with Twitter and Ocean Outdoor


The #LIVETRENDS interface was designed in-house by TOPSHOP and custom-built by Stackla for the outdoor billboards. Using the Stackla API, we integrated trend data from Twitter with product data from TOPSHOPs own systems to create the billboards dynamically. The dynamic and live content was also featured across all customer touchpoints including topshop.com at the TOPSHOP Showspace at London Fashion Week, in regional stores across the UK, at the Oxford Circus flagship store, and on Twitter.
